# Code of Conduct

LexiRay follows the Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct, version 2.1.

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for
everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability,
ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of
experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance,
race, caste, color, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and direct.
- Keep technical feedback specific and actionable.
- Assume good intent, but correct unsafe or inaccurate work clearly.
- Respect the clean-room boundary and project scope.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, insults, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Sexualized language or attention.
- Deliberately introducing copied, incompatible, or undisclosed third-party code.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject contributions, or ban
participants who violate this code.
